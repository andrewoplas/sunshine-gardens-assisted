Services

Standard Services

Standard Services are offered for the enjoyment of all residents.

  • Three Chef-Prepared Meals Daily, Plus Snacks Available Throughout the Day
  • Housekeeping
  • Laundry Room
  • Assistance with Activities of Daily Living
  • Scheduled Transportation
  • Private Dining Area for Entertaining Family and Friends
  • Emergency Response Device (Programmed Personally to Each Resident)
  • Resident and Family Care Plan Meetings
  • 24/7 Staffing
  • Group Exercise Program
  • Wellness Program
  • Religious Services
  • In-House Store for Convenience Items
  • Full Access to Community Common Areas: Exercise Room, Arts and Crafts Room, Movie Theater, Chapel and Gardens

Customized Services

Customized Services are ala carte and based upon the level of care required or desired by each resident. Separate Charges Apply and are Set by Service Provider.

  • Massage Therapist
  • Physical Therapist
  • Beauty/Barber Shop
  • Personal Phone Line within the Sunshine Gardens Phone System
  • Internet Offered at a Reduced Rate

Dining

Too many older adults, especially those who live alone, miss meals and don’t receive proper nutrition. The chefs at Sunshine Gardens use only the highest quality fresh ingredients to prepare tastefully prepared meals. Broad menu choices are offered and dietary restrictions are observed.

The community dining area is situated alongside the indoor gardens and fountains – a delight for the senses. Residents can enjoy nutritious meals while socializing with their neighbors or simply relaxing over a morning cup of coffee and newspaper.

Outings

Scheduled transportation makes it easy for residents to shop, make medical appointments and enjoy community outings as a group.

Health Services

Our staff is available to assist residents with medication reminders. Residents who need a medical provider to come to their home will enjoy the convenience of our onsite medical exam rooms. The community will also host wellness education seminars and other health-related education.

Life Gets Easier Here ………

Perhaps the biggest benefit of being a resident at Sunshine Gardens is the ability to enjoy life without the stress of everyday tasks such as home maintenance, yard upkeep, meal preparation and cleaning.

It’s much easier to get excited about waking up to a beautiful environment, gourmet meals and stimulating activities. And, maintaining good health is a cornerstone to enjoying all of those things.

It’s easy to wake up and get out of bed when you know you can enjoy a cup of coffee and a newspaper in an indoor garden even when the weather outside is cold and snowy. It’s easy to keep going when you know your friend will come by and join you for a card game or Bingo.

It’s easy to get excited about the day if you enjoy a good Karaoke night with friends. It’s easy to be happy if you know you can enjoy relaxing if your community is filled with beauty and your home is always comfortable and inviting.

Come experience Sunshine Gardens where every day is full of relaxation. You’ll learn to laugh again, socialize, learn a new skill, make new friends and invite old friends in for a visit. It’s a great place to celebrate life!
