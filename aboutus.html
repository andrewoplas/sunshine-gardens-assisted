About Us

Sunshine Gardens is one of the largest independently owned assisted living facilities in Southern Illinois offering full spectrum of services at an affordable fee.

We recognize that each individual has his or her own personal needs and we focus on them and develop individualized care program based on the individual needs of each of our residents.

Our residents are provided with a balance of independence along with the appropriate level of care and services they require within a very supportive environment. This means that our residents get to choose what they want to do, when they want to do it and to receive daily assistance only as needed.
