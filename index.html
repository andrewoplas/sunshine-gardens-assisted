Life Gets Easier From Here

Older adults in Southern Illinois now have a community designed for a carefree lifestyle. Sunshine Gardens is one of the largest independently-owned senior communities in the area that offers a full spectrum of services including nutritious meals, socialization and programs to stimulate the mind and body.
Sunshine Gardens provides a balance of independent living along with personal help with Activities of Daily Living (ADL). We are committed to providing a safe and peaceful community where residents can experience an enriched quality of life.
